# consumer-insights-platform
Guest insights platform that combines survey responses, reviews text, loyalty behavior, product feedback,a dn store-level data to identify the highest-impact opportunities for improving customer experince, brand perception, and repeat visits.
Business questions:
What are the most common Voice of Customer themes?
Which themes are most associated with low NPS (Net Promoter Score) and low revisit intent?
How do pain points differ by guest segment, channel, region, and store type?
Which stores should leadership prioritize?
What action has the clearest $100K+ upside?
